Each learning block consists of modules delivered through asynchronous and live sessions, all aligned to the method below.

LED
Method
L
Learn — Async
Completed at least 24 hours before the session. Includes pre-recorded videos, articles & readings, and case studies so staff arrive with foundational knowledge, not a blank slate.
E
Engage — Facilitator-Led Session
Builds on pre-work. Flow: quick recap of key takeaways → deep dive with real-world scenarios → interactive elements (polls, knowledge checks, small group discussion).
D
Deepen — Guided Panel Discussion
Facilitator introduces panelists → 4–6 pre-prepared guided questions → panelist open floor → participant Q&A → wrap-up with key takeaways and a challenge to apply the learning.
