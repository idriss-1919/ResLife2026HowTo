UCLA Residential Life · Summer Training Architecture
Become a PRO
Not just trained. Connected, confident, and prepared.
The “Become a PRO” framework is the architecture behind everything you will experience during summer training.

It is organized around our three pillars (ResLife Welcomes, Connects, and Responds) and built through the PRO model (Philosophy, Roles, Operations). You will move through each dimension with intention, building from the why behind the work, to who you are in this system, to how you execute with excellence.

Every async module, every live session, and every capstone experience is designed to bring you closer to one outcome: not just trained, but connected, confident, and prepared.

LED
Method
L
Learn — Async
Completed at least 24 hours before the session. Includes pre-recorded videos, articles & readings, case studies, and reflection questions so staff arrive with foundational knowledge, not a blank slate.
E
Engage — Facilitator-Led Session
Builds on pre-work and never repeats it. Flow: quick recap of key takeaways → deep dive with real-world scenarios → interactive elements (polls, knowledge checks, small group discussion).
D
Deepen — Guided Panel Discussion
Facilitator introduces panelists → 4–6 pre-prepared guided questions → panelist open floor → participant Q&A → wrap-up with key takeaways and a challenge to apply the learning.
