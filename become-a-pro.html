The PROgression

Within each pillar, learning follows three steps, always in that order. Why before who. Who before how.

P

Philosophy

Why does this matter?

Start with purpose. Every 100-series builds the philosophical foundation first.

R

Roles

Who's involved?

Locate yourself. Every 200-series defines where you fit and what's yours to carry.

O

Operations

How do I do this?

Apply it. Every 300-series turns philosophy and role clarity into daily execution.

Two training phases

Department training builds the foundation. Unit training builds the rooms.

1
Phase One

Department Training

Everyone learns together before specializing. Same language, same expectations, same approach across all three pillars. This is where the foundation is built.

2
Phase Two

Unit Training

Each team builds role-specific knowledge under each pillar, culminating in a capstone (Unit 399) where staff practice decisions through real-world scenarios.

The LED Method

Three stages that move participants from foundational knowledge to expert insight.

L

Learn

Build foundational knowledge so participants arrive prepared.

Completed 24 hrs before
  • Articles and readings
  • Pre-recorded videos
  • Real-world scenarios
E

Engage

Reinforce foundational knowledge and align understanding.

Facilitator-led live session
  • Learn recap
  • Deep dive presentation
  • Interactive polls, small discussions
D

Deepen

Bring in real-world expertise through a guided panel.

Expert panel + Q&A
  • Facilitator introduces panelists
  • Guided panel discussion
  • Participant Q&A
