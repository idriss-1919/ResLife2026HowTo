Part 2 — The LED Method

Every live training session at ResLife follows the Learn → Engage → Deepen approach — three phases that move participants from foundational knowledge to real-world application to expert insight.

L
Phase 1
Learn
Build foundational knowledge so participants arrive prepared.
⏰ Completed 24 hrs before session
  • Articles, readings, and key background concepts
  • Pre-recorded videos — lectures, interviews, case studies
  • Real-world scenarios to analyze in advance
  • Reflection questions or quizzes to focus attention
E
Phase 2
Engage
Reinforce foundational knowledge, align understanding, and set the stage for deeper discussion.
🗣️ Facilitator-led live session
  • Welcome & quick recap of pre-work key takeaways
  • Deep dive presentation — expand with real-world examples
  • Interactive elements: polls, knowledge checks, small discussions
  • Build on pre-work rather than repeat it
D
Phase 3
Deepen
Bring in real-world expertise through a guided panel and open Q&A.
🎙️ Expert panel + Q&A
  • Facilitator introduces panelists with brief bios and relevance
  • Guided panel discussion — 4–6 pre-prepared questions
  • Open floor: panelists share insights, challenges, best practices
  • Participant Q&A — facilitator holds one backup question
  • Wrap-up: key insights and a challenge to apply learning
Why the LED Method?
Progressive Learning
Builds from foundational knowledge to application to expert insight — each phase unlocks the next.
Active Engagement
Participants don't just consume — they interact, apply, and ask meaningful questions at each stage.
Expert-Driven Insights
Real-world application comes from those with lived experience, not just the curriculum.
